About us

Laksh Overseas Consultancy serves as your comprehensive hub for international education needs and work visas. Our primary goal is to guide students in making optimal decisions about pursuing education at foreign institutions. We offer valuable insights into higher education across diverse countries, including USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, Dubai, Europe, Germany, France, Malta, Latvia, Poland, Spain, Greece, Austria, Lithuania, Sweden, Hungary, and Cyprus.
